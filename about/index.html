Get to Know the Girl Behind the Code

Hometown

Born and raised on the southside of Chicago, Illinois. I lived in the Back of the Yards neighborhood until the age of 10. My family relocated to the Garfield Ridge area, also referred to as Midway. I still live there to this day. Chicago is all I know, but I plan to travel away from my home roots upon graduation in 2019!

Sports Teams

I wholeheartedly believe that an individual cannot be both a Sox fan and Cubs fan. The whole point of living in this city is picking a team to keep the rivalry alive. Therefore, Sox pride all the way! I'm also a Bears fan, a Bulls fan, and a Blackhawks fan. Did I mention I have worked for a security company who was hired for Bears games, Sox games, and Cubs games? Yes, it was awesome- even when I worked the Cubs games because I got to live-up the cross-town rivalry.

Astrology

I was born on September 28th, so my zodiac sign is the Libra; however, my moon is a Virgo! The symbol for Libra is the scales. Therefore, I am always looking at both sides of the spectrum. Disclaimer: I do not think that everything in your life happens as a result of your zodiac sign, but I do believe some characteristics apply to individuals depending on when they were born.
